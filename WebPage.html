What Is Human Consciousness?

Consciousness is the state of being aware of and able to think about yourself, your surroundings, your thoughts, and experiences.

It includes:

  • Awareness: Knowing what’s going on around you and within you.
  • Subjectivity: Having personal experiences (the "what it feels like" part).
  • Intentionality: The mind's ability to direct itself toward something (a thought, an object, a memory).
  • Self-awareness: Recognizing yourself as an individual, separate from others.
